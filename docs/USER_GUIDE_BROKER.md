# Broker / Dealer User Guide

Welcome to the BondKonnect Broker module. This guide helps you manage liquidity and execute trades on the platform.

## 1. Managing the Quote Book

As a Broker, your primary tool is the **Quote Book**. You provide liquidity by posting Bids (buying) and Offers (selling).

### Posting a New Quote
1.  Navigate to the **Market** or **Quotes** section.
2.  Click **"Create Quote"**.
3.  Select the **Bond Issue** (e.g., FXD1/2023/10Yr).
4.  Enter your **Yield** and **Price**. The system will automatically calculate the dirty price for you.
5.  Set the **Amount** you are willing to trade.
6.  **Submit.** Your quote is now live and visible to all traders.

### Activating & Suspending Quotes
*   **Active:** Quotes remain active for 3 business days by default.
*   **Suspend:** If you want to temporarily remove a quote from the market without deleting it, use the **Suspend** action.
*   **Re-activate:** You can re-activate a suspended or expired quote to put it back on the board.

---

## 2. Handling Transactions

When a trader "hits" your quote, a transaction is initiated.

### Accepting a Trade
1.  Go to **My Transactions**.
2.  Review the incoming request (Amount, Price, Counterparty).
3.  Click **Accept** to proceed to settlement or **Reject** if the terms are no longer valid.
4.  Once the trade is settled offline/via CBK, mark the transaction as **Settled** to update portfolios.

### Partial Fills
If a trader requests an amount larger than your quote, the system will:
1.  Fill the transaction up to your quoted limit.
2.  Automatically generate a **New Quote** for the remaining balance, which you can then review and activate.

---

## 3. Trust & Credibility

Your **Credibility Badge** (Platinum, Gold, Silver) is visible to all traders. 
*   Maintain a high rating by responding quickly to transaction requests.
*   Ensure timely settlement to avoid negative feedback and score decay.
