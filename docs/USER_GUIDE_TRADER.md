# Trader / Investor User Guide

Welcome to BondKonnect. This guide will walk you through bond discovery, analytics, and trade execution.

## 1. Bond Discovery & Analytics

The **Market Dashboard** provides a real-time view of the Kenyan bond market.

### Using the Bond Calculator
Before trading, use the **Bond Calculator** to understand your potential returns:
1.  Select a bond from the list.
2.  Enter a **Target Yield** or **Target Price**.
3.  The calculator will provide:
    *   **Dirty Price:** The total cost including interest.
    *   **Accrued Interest:** The interest earned since the last coupon.
    *   **Modified Duration:** A measure of the bond's price sensitivity to interest rate changes.

### Research Assistant
Use the **AI Research Assistant** to ask questions about market trends, specific bond issuances, or help with platform navigation.

---

## 2. Trading in the Secondary Market

### "Hitting" a Quote
When you find a favorable Bid or Offer in the market:
1.  Click on the quote to open the **Transaction Dialog**.
2.  Enter the **Amount** you wish to trade.
3.  The system will show you the total consideration (Receivable/Payable).
4.  **Submit.** The broker will be notified immediately to accept or reject your request.

---

## 3. Portfolio Management

Once your trades are settled, they appear in your **Portfolio Analytics**.

### Tracking Performance
*   **Unrealized PnL:** See how your portfolio value changes based on current market spot rates.
*   **Cash Flow Calendar:** View upcoming coupon payments and maturity dates.
*   **Risk Metrics:** Monitor your portfolio's weighted average duration and risk concentration.

---

## 4. Trusting Your Counterparty

Always check a Broker's **Trust Indicator** before trading.
*   **Gold/Platinum:** Highly reliable with a strong track record.
*   **Bronze/Unrated:** Exercise caution or verify details via the chat module.
