# BondKonnect AI Concierge: User Guide

Welcome to the **BondKonnect AI Concierge**, your 24/7 personal assistant for fixed-income trading and platform navigation.

## 🌟 What is the AI Concierge?
Unlike standard chatbots, our Concierge is specifically trained on the **BondKonnect Ecosystem** and the **Kenyan Bond Market**. It doesn't give general internet advice—it gives precise, platform-verified directions and financial context.

---

## 🚀 How to Access
Look for the **Sparkles (✨)** icon at the bottom right of your dashboard.
- **Click to Open:** Toggle the chat window anytime.
- **Always Active:** The assistant follows you as you navigate between the Marketplace, Portfolio, and Billing.

---

## 💡 What Can You Ask?

### 1. Platform Navigation
- *"How do I deposit money with M-Pesa?"*
- *"Where can I see my P&L?"*
- *"How do I upgrade to the Institutional Terminal?"*

### 2. Market Education (Kenyan Bonds)
- *"What is an IFB bond and is it taxed?"*
- *"Explain the difference between FXD and SSDK."*
- *"What are the current withholding tax rates for treasury bonds?"*

### 3. Financial Analysis
- *"Explain what Convexity and Duration mean for my portfolio."*
- *"How do I use the YTM Calculator?"*

---

## 🛠 Features for a Better Experience

### 📍 Context Awareness
The AI knows which page you are on. If you are on the **Bond Stats** page and ask *"What is this?"*, it will automatically explain the bond statistics table and its metrics.

### 🔗 Actionable Links
When the AI gives you directions, it often provides **clickable links**. For example, it might say:
> "You can manage your primary and secondary funding nodes in the **[Billing Section](/dashboard/apps/billing)**."
Click the bold link to navigate directly to that page.

### ⚡ Quick Suggestions
At the start of every chat, we provide one-tap buttons for our most common workflows. This helps you get answers without typing!

---

## 🛡 Disclaimer
The AI Concierge is a tool for information and navigation. It does not provide definitive financial advice. Always consult with a certified financial advisor before making large investment decisions.
